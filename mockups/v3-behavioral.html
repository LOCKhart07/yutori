① Traffic-light dashboard hero — pace-driven

Bucket isn't raw % of budget; it's (actual % used) − (% of month elapsed). Same 32% used reads green on day 25 and warn-orange on day 5 — the colour tells you whether your pace is sustainable, not just how full the tank is.

Buckets: green ≤ −10pp (under-pace, surplus brewing) · neutral ±10pp (on track) · warn-orange +10 to +25pp or 80–100% raw · red > +25pp or ≥ 100%. Each ring shows a faint tick at "today's expected position" so the user can see pace at a glance — the gap between fill and tick is the surplus/deficit story.

② Post-spend "impact" notification

Per-transaction push that fires when a single SPEND is ≥ X% of the monthly effective budget (default 10%, configurable, off by default). Distinct from the existing cumulative-threshold alerts (50/80/100% of total spend). The push lands within seconds of the SMS so the user gets the impact moment after the swipe but before they've forgotten. Tap → opens TransactionDetail. The settings card on the right shows where the threshold lives.
