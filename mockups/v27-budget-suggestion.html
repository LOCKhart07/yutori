Behaviour contract

  • When shown: on BudgetSetup whenever medianPriorNetSpend != null (≥1 prior month with spend) and the chip hasn't been used/dismissed this session — independent of whether the field is empty, inherited, or a saved value. This is the "accessible every time" change vs. the original XOR-with-inheritance spec.
  • Whole chip is the tap target. The trailing "Use" pill is a visual affordance, not the only hit region.
  • Suppressed when redundant: if the field value already equals the suggestion, the chip is hidden (no no-op nag).
  • Median, not mean — discards a single spike month in the window. Multi-month seasonal stretches and "budgeting the peak month itself" can't be solved without year-over-year history (we don't have ≥13 months yet); the visible per-month breakdown is the safety net until then, and a YoY blend is a data-gated v2 hook.
  • Onboarding excluded — the step-3 historical import is async and hasn't populated transactions by the step-4 budget prompt, so the median would be null there anyway. Scope is BudgetSetupScreen (dashboard CTA / month editing) only.

Open questions

  • Show the per-month breakdown? Mockup shows "Mar 40k · Apr 45k · May 42k" in the basis line — this is the transparency that lets you spot a seasonal month. Drop to just "median of last 3 months" if it reads too busy. (Pairs with #42.)
  • Colour split — green chip vs periwinkle inheritance note. Alternative: make both periwinkle and rely on the "Use" pill + arrow to signal tappability. Green was chosen to read "actionable / a number to grab."
  • Lead-in copy — "Suggested" / "Or use". Alt: always "Suggested", let context carry it.
