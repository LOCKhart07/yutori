Design decisions baked in — flag any you'd flip:

1. Notes promoted out of MetaRow into a full-width card. Fixes the pre-existing readability issue with multi-line notes. Means MetaSection at TransactionDetailScreen.kt:298 loses its tx.notes?.let { ... } row.
2. Action row as forward scaffold. Shows 3 slots even though only 1 is wired — so when #27-part-2 (Ignore) and #29 (Add rule) land, no re-layout. Ghost buttons in v1 are deliberately disabled, not hidden — signals the roadmap without shipping half a feature. Could alternatively hide them entirely until wired; say the word.
3. Bottom sheet, not inline. Multi-line editor on a scroll-heavy screen is easier in a sheet than inline. Matches existing editor patterns in the app (BudgetSetup, AccountEdit).
4. 500-char cap. Plucked from the triage comment. Long enough for "dentist appointment — follow-up for filling, partial reimbursement coming from insurance" with room to spare. Could tighten to 280 if we want a harder nudge.
5. No keyword-reclassify offer in v1. The triage comment suggests an "Sounds like Groceries — reclassify?" prompt on save. Holding that for the auto-rule thingy pass (#29), since we'd rather ship the cleanest version of that flow once than a half-featured shadow of it now.
